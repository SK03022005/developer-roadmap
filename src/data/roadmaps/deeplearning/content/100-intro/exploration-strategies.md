## Exploration Strategies in Reinforcement Learning resource


### what is exploration Strategies in Reinforcement Learning resource?
Exploration Strategies in Reinforcement Learning refer to techniques that agents use to actively explore their environment to gather information and learn about their surroundings. In Reinforcement Learning, agents must learn through trial and error, which means that they need to try different actions and observe their outcomes to improve their decision-making process.
